About me

I'm 15 years old. since I'm a kid, I'm interested in coding. I went from hardware (to build a lot of heavy projects) to very basic electronics and soldering. After this small introduction to electronics, I fell in love with high voltage! I started with a bad "400kV" aliExpress high voltage module. I found a CRT TV a month later, and extracted the flyback transformer. I drove it with a pretty weak driver, but I did a ZVS driver afterward. I finally started playing with MOTs. I have done some Jacobs's Ladder, Lichtenberg Figures, Tesla coils and I'm still not dead!
